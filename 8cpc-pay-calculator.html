About 8th CPC Calculator

This calculator provides an estimate of your potential salary under the proposed 8th Central Pay Commission based on various assumptions and historical patterns from previous pay commissions.

The calculations are based on:

  • Projected fitment factor (2.5x to 3.2x)
  • Current DA rate (46%)
  • Standard HRA classification (30%, 20%, 10%) or custom HRA rate
  • Approximate deduction rates for NPS, taxes, etc.

Note: This is only an estimation tool. Actual 8th CPC recommendations may vary.
