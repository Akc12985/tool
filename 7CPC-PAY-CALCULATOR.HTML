About 7th CPC

The 7th Central Pay Commission (CPC) was implemented on January 1, 2016, revising salaries and pensions for central government employees.

Key features include:

  • Minimum pay set at ₹18,000
  • Maximum pay at ₹2,50,000
  • Pay matrix with 18 levels
  • HRA rates revised to 27%, 18% and 9%
