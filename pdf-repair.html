PDF Repair Tips
  • Make a backup - Always keep a copy of your original file before attempting repairs
  • Try different viewers - Sometimes the issue is with your PDF reader, not the file itself
  • Check for encryption - Password-protected files may require decryption first
  • Update your software - Older PDF readers might have trouble with newer PDF versions
